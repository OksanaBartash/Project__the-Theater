I, Daria Vardanyan. I was born in Toretsk, Donetsk region.

In 2010, I started my education at the Donetsk School of Culture, where I specialized in directing mass spectacles and celebrations.

Later in 2014, I enrolled in the Kyiv Municipal Academy of Circus and Variety Arts, where I developed my skills in a variety of art directing. Finally, in 2019, I received my master's degree.

Throughout her studies, I was involved in various projects.

-The Children's Fashion Theater called "Little Model", where I worked as a teacher of acting, fashion shows, and choreography for children.

-I was the director, actor, and set designer for the U! ZAHVATI Company where I worked on projects such as the promenade performance called "Time" and an interactive fairy tale for children named "Gulyandia".

-2020, I had the opportunity to work at the Hungarian National Circus in Budapest. Currently,

-I hold the position of chief director at the children's theater studio called "The Theater" since 2020.
