My name is Vladyslava Khlop'cha, and I was born on February 7th, 2004 in Vinnytsia.

I attended Secondary School No. 12, completing 11 grades. In 2010, I began my dancing career, competing in various events, and qualifying for the Ukrainian Hip-Hop Championship.

Currently, I am a university student, a member of the Rhythm of the Heart folk group, and a modern choreography teacher at Ilona Dance Studio.
