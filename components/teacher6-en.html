Klitinska Olga

I am a vocal teacher and bandura player. I also perform as an artist with the Leontovych Chapel Choir at the Vinnytsia Regional Philharmonic.

I have won both international and national competitions. In terms of education, I graduated from the Leontovych Vinnytsia College of Culture and Arts in 2020, where I studied bandura. Currently, I am finishing my studies at Kotsiubynskyi Vinnytsia Pedagogical University. I am pursuing a double degree in 025 Musical Art (academic vocals) and 014.13 Musical Art (secondary education).
