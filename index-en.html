Our teachers

Karen

The head of the theater

My name is Vardanian Karen Karenovych. I was born in Vinnytsia in the winter of 1995.

In 4th grade, I acted in my first school play, and from that moment, I aspired to become an actor.

In 2014, he enrolled in the Faculty of Theater and Film at Ivan Karpenko-Kary Kyiv National University of Theater, Cinema and Television.I pursued a Master's degree in 2018 to further my studies.

I have corrected any spelling, grammar, and punctuation errors.

He graduated with honors from both of his degrees. I began teaching in Kyiv in 2016 but always dreamed of opening my own studio in my hometown.

In 2020, my dream finally came true, and now my children and I are able to dream together.
